Root Chakra — Muladhara

Stability & safety
I am grounded and secure.

This page offers a short healing practice for the root chakra focusing on yoga poses and breath to bring balance.

Pose: Mountain Pose (Tadasana)

  1. Begin in a comfortable standing or seated position.
  2. Move mindfully into mountain pose (tadasana) following your breath.
  3. Hold for 3–7 breaths, focusing on the area of the root chakra.
  4. Release slowly and rest with attentive breath.

Tip: Combine with the affirmation above. Repeat it silently with each inhale.
