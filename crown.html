Crown Chakra

Action: Accept silence.
Practice Saying: Om.

Pose: Lotus Pose

  1. Begin in a comfortable seated position.
  2. Move mindfully into lotus pose. Lotus pose is cross legged position. This may take time to get used to - go with the flow.
  3. Hold for three to seven breaths, focusing on the area of the crown chakra. Follow your breathe as you inhale, and exhale.
  4. Release slowly and rest with gratitude.

Tip: Combine with the affirmation above. Repeat it silently with each inhale.
