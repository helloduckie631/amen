Third Eye Chakra — Ajna

Insight & clarity
I am intuitive and worthy of mental clarity.

This page offers a short healing practice for the third eye chakra focusing on yoga poses and breath to bring balance.

Pose: Child's Pose (Balasana)

  1. Begin in a comfortable standing or seated position.
  2. Move mindfully into child's pose (balasana) following your breath.
  3. Hold for 3–7 breaths, focusing on the area of the third eye chakra.
  4. Release slowly and rest with attentive breath.

Tip: Combine with the affirmation above. Repeat it silently with each inhale.
